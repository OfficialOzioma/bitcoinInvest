Why Do I Need to Verify My ID?

Answer:

Why am I being asked to verify my identity?

ID verification is required to prevent fraud and keep the community safe. It also adds an extra layer of security by ensuring no one but you have access to your account. In some cases, we require an additional verification in order to enable or continue investing with us.

Why the Delay in Completing ID Verification?

ID verification is usually completed within 7 to 10 working days. However in some cases it may be longer as a result of the following reasons:

  • Late response from the authorities of the ID issuing country confirming the authenticity of the document.
  • Inability to read the details on the documents.
  • Providing forged ID documnets

When uploading your photo ID through Email attachment

  • Ensure that your document is valid and not expired, without hole punches or other modifications
  • Ensure your document is in a well-lit area without glare. Natural sunlight is best
  • Photograph the entire document and avoid cutting off any corners or sides
  • Ensure the ID is fully visible and in focus
